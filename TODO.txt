- Learning phases:
    1 Recognition: foreign word, English word, image, listened foreign word, listened English word
    2 Recall: foreign word, English word, image, listened foreign word, listened English word
    3 Recite: Rewrite foreign sentence string from memory, write foreign sentence from listened sentence
    4 Translation: Translate foreign sentence string to English, translate English sentence string to foreign language, translate foreign sentence listened to English, translate English sentence listened to foreign language
- Repetition with increasing delays
    - Through a session
    - Across sessions
- Chunk learning into groups of 7 words
- Immediate feedback
- Train in the range that is medium, not too easy, not too hard
    - Have a user adjustable temperature for if they want to make it harder or easier
    - Have a user data based computed difficulty estimate
- Word mastery categories
    - 0: Not encountered
    - 1: Bad recognition
    - 2: Good recognition
    ---------------------------------
    - 0: Not encountered
    - 1: Bad recall
    - 2: Okay recall
    - 3: Good recall
    ---------------------------------
    - 0: Not encountered
    - 1: Bad sentence integration
    - 2: Good sentence integration
    ---------------------------------
    - 0: Not encountered
    - 1: Bad translation integration
    - 2: Okay translation integration
    - 3: Good translation integration
- Word mastery level changes
    - Correct recognition: +1
    - Incorrect recognition: -1
    - Correct recall: +1
    - Incorrect recall: -2
    - Correct sentence integration: +1
    - Incorrect sentence integration: -1
    - Correct translation integration: +1
    - Incorrect translation integration: -2
- Word mastery long term repetition
    - 1 day
    - 2 days
    - 4 days
    - 1 week
    - 2 weeks
    - 1 month
    - 2 months
    - 4 months
    - 6 months
    - 1 year
    - 2 years
- Word mastery long term repetition rules
    - Each category is reset to 1 after each repetition milestone for each word
- Within each session, use pomodoro timing, 25 mins on, 5 mins off


- Minigames
    - Given foreign word, choose from list of English translations
    - Given English word, choose from list of foreign translations
    - Given image, choose from list of foreign translations
    - Listen to foreign word, choose from list of foreign words
    - Listen to foreign word, choose from list of English words
    - Listen to English word, choose from list of foreign words

    - Given foreign word, type the English translation
    - Given English word, type the foreign translation
    - Given image, type the foreign translation
    - Listen to foreign word, type the foreign word
    - Listen to foreign word, type the English word
    - Listen to English word, type the foreign word

    - Given foreign sentence, type the foreign sentence
    - Listen to foreign sentence, type the foreign sentence

    - Given foreign sentence, type the English sentence
    - Given English sentence, type the foreign sentence
    - Listen to foreign sentence, type the English sentence
    - Listen to English sentence, type the foreign sentence

LLM prompts:
- Respond with a single sentence in <language> only allowing these words: [<randomize list of words for variety>]
